About Me

Hi, I'm Chris. After being let go from my job and experiencing a lot of personal loss, I found solace in creating unique art. What started as a hobby to cope with difficult times has now become a passion that I want to share with everyone across the country.

At Dragonwood Designs, we specialize in creating one-of-a-kind art pieces that combine the beauty of animal skulls and bones with the elegance of driftwood resin. We also craft unique bone jewelry, animal tooth jewelry, and handmade copper jewelry. Each piece is handcrafted with care, making it a perfect gift for any occasion. Our eco-friendly designs are inspired by fantasy worlds, ensuring that each item is unique and captivating.

FAQ

What is animal skull and bone art?

Animal skull and bone art involves using natural bone materials to create unique, artistic designs. Each piece is one-of-a-kind and showcases the natural beauty of the bones.

How do you ensure your materials are eco-friendly?

We source our bones from ethical suppliers and use non-toxic, eco-friendly resins in our creations. Our commitment to the environment is a core part of our business.
